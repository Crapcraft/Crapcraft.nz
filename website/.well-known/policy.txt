crapcraft.nz Security Policy
=============================

The operator of crapcraft.nz is a hobbyist with limited security expertise.
Any assistance, responsible disclosure, or advice regarding the security of
this platform is genuinely appreciated.

If you find a vulnerability or have security concerns, please contact:

  Email: admin@crapcraft.nz
  Discord: @waayer (prefered so we can actually reply to you)

We will do our best to respond promptly and appreciate your patience.

No bug bounty is offered - this is a personal, non-commercial project.
Please do not attempt destructive testing. If you're not sure whether
something is okay, just ask first.
